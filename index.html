Why Choose Our Music School in Yelahanka?

Shree Sangeetha Aalaya is Yelahanka's leading Carnatic music academy, dedicated to preserving and teaching traditional Indian classical music. Located conveniently in Prestige Monte Carlo, Yelahanka, we serve students from across North Bangalore including Jakkur, Sahakara Nagar, Hebbal, and surrounding areas.

Flexible Learning Options: We understand the busy schedules of modern families. That's why we offer both online and offline music classes in Bangalore, ensuring you can learn Carnatic music at your convenience - whether you're in Yelahanka or anywhere in the world.

Frequently Asked Questions - Music Classes in Yelahanka

Where is Shree Sangeetha Aalaya located in Yelahanka?

We are located in Prestige Monte Carlo, Yelahanka, Bangalore. Easily accessible from Yelahanka New Town, Old Town, Jakkur, Sahakara Nagar, and Hebbal.

Do you offer online Carnatic music classes?

Yes! We offer both online and offline music classes. You can attend in-person at our Yelahanka center or join our online classes from anywhere in Bangalore or worldwide.

What is the fee for music classes in Yelahanka?

Our fees are competitive and vary based on the program (1-on-1 or group) and duration. We offer special discounts for 3, 6, and 12-month packages. Contact us for detailed pricing.

Can adults join your Carnatic music classes?

Absolutely! We have programs for all age groups - kids, teenagers, and adults. It's never too late to start learning Carnatic music.
